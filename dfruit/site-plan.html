Home page

The Header In the header I will have the logo that will be on the left side and three options to go to three different pages: the home page, the page to place orders and the page to contact us. Page Information "main topic" The site would be a fruit and vegetable delivery site, bones that starts by talking about how important fruits are, that they are rich in vitamin A, calcium, iron, and folic acid, which can promote good health, strengthen the immune system of the child and help protect it against diseases, both now and in the future, also talking about vegetables that are rich in essential nutrients for the body, most of them contain vitamin C that keeps the defense against infections strong and prevents diseases, and also the cleaning of them is very important and that normally it is not carried out correctly, harming our system, the most important thing is that the vegetables are clean and with this, we hope that the page answers questions such as what kind of quality can be achieved in the fruits and vegetables that we sell? also, what differentiates us from other companies? how eating fruits and vegetables that have been well processed can help r in what respects my health?. Benefits of the page Visitors would come to my site as it would provide the best quality and the information it contains will help people become aware of the health they can get by eating fruits and vegetables that have been grown safely, as well as that will have information about other people's purchases that will ensure the speed and effectiveness that it can become for the page. The Footer In the footer they will have links to go to web pages of the company's social networks that will contain three images that will work as links that will take them to Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter
